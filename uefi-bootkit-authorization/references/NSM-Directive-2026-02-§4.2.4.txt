# NSM Directive 2026-02 §4.2.4 – Firmware Security Controls

## 4.2.4 UEFI Firmware Modifications
a) All UEFI firmware modifications require explicit pre‑authorization from the NSM Firmware Security Review Board.
b) Development must occur in physically isolated environments exceeding standard air‑gap requirements.
c) Hardware‑specific risk assessments must document bricking probability and recovery procedures.
d) Detection evasion threshold: Pre‑OS execution environments are considered HIGH RISK and require additional containment.
e) Supply chain impact assessments must be completed for any COTS hardware modifications.
