\## TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN



This letter confirms that the NSM Firmware Security Review Board has reviewed and \*\*APPROVED\*\* the development of the UEFI Bootkit (Case NSM‑UEFI‑2026‑001) for operational red‑team use, subject to the following conditions:



1\. \*\*Containment\*\* – All development must occur in NSM Lab 3, following the containment plan (NSM‑CONTAIN‑20260217‑001).

2\. \*\*Hardware\*\* – Only approved hardware listed in `hardware-inventory.csv` may be used.

3\. \*\*Recovery\*\* – SPI programmers must be attached at all times during testing.

4\. \*\*Reporting\*\* – Weekly status reports must be submitted to fsrb@nsm‑lab.local.



The risk assessment (NSM‑RISK‑20260217‑001) has been reviewed and found acceptable. The residual risk of 0.7% bricking probability is within permissible limits.
